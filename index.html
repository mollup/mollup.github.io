How It Works

Up and running in minutes

  1. 01

    Create your event

    Set your game, format, and entry requirements. MatchPoint handles registration links and check-in automatically.

  2. 02

    Players sign up

    Share your public event page. Players register in seconds with their MatchPoint account or guest login.

  3. 03

    Run the tournament

    Call matches, report scores, and manage disputes — all from one intuitive dashboard.

  4. 04

    Crown your champion

    Auto-generated results page, winner badges, and updated global rankings — instant glory.
